Dear Codam students!

The Student Council would like to present you with a list of candidates running for the coming term. The 4 winning ones will be representing you for the next 8 months, from August until March 2022.

The elections will be held in the week of the 19nd - 25th of July. You will receive a link to a voting system in which you can rank the candidates to your liking. You can also choose to abstain. For this term we are testing preferential/ranked voting to get a fairer method of fixing ties, you can read about that on this electionbuddy page. It is very important for us and the candidates that you vote. Your participation gives us the legitimacy to represent you. Please get to know the candidates and their motivation to join the Student Council.

What is a Student Council?

A Student Council is a delegation of students within a school that serves the interests of students at that school. The Student Council at Codam consists of six students. These students represent the entire student body in conversations with (delegates) of the Bocal. The Student Council is built to support students by speaking to the Bocal on their behalf, generate ideas to improve education and life in general at Codam and improve the connection between the students and the Bocal. The Student Councilâ€™s goal is to help Codam provide its students with the best possible education. Areas the Student Council can be focussing on are, for example, education quality (e.g. curriculum and evaluations), student support (financial and mental), facilities, and admissions (new students, piscines, selection). We strive to have a team as diverse as possible with regards to cohort, nationality, gender etc.
